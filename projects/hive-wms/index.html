Machine Learning
7-Model ML Subsystem
Model A

Travel Time Prediction

LightGBM · Gradient Boosting

Predicts travel time for pick operations based on distance, lift position, and time of day. Enables route optimization.

Model B

Kanban Demand Forecasting

Prophet · Time Series

7-day forward demand forecast per material based on historical consumption patterns and seasonality.

Model C

Job Duration Prediction

RandomForest · GradientBoosting

Estimates picking job duration from job size, item count, and travel distance. Improves scheduling accuracy.

Model D

Anomaly Detection

IsolationForest · Unsupervised

Detects unusual transaction patterns in real-time. Flags potential errors, fraud, or system issues.

Model E

Zone Congestion Prediction

Custom · Real-Time

Predicts warehouse zone congestion based on concurrent active jobs. Enables proactive load balancing.

Model F

KPI Time Series Forecasting

Prophet · 30-Day Trend

Forward-looking KPI projections for management planning. Accuracy tracking and model refresh.

Model G

Lift Maintenance Prediction

Custom · Cycles/Errors

Predicts automated lift maintenance needs based on cycle count, error rate, and age. Reduces unplanned downtime.
