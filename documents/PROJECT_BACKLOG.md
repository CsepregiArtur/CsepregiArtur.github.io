# Risk Register

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Scope creep | Follow roadmap. Stick to milestones. |
| Too many animations | Executive design only. No distractions. |
| Content inconsistency | Follow CONTENT_STRATEGY.md and COPYWRITING_MASTER.md. |
| Poor mobile experience | Mobile testing after every milestone. |
| Confidential information | Anonymize all project data. No client names. |
