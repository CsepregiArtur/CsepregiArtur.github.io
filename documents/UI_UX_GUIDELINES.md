# Executive Portfolio Website
## User Experience & User Interface Guidelines

Version: 1.0

Owner: Artur Csepregi

---

# 1. UX Philosophy

The website should not feel like a developer portfolio.

It should feel like visiting the website of a Manufacturing Executive.

The visitor should immediately perceive:

• Professionalism
• Confidence
• Leadership
• Innovation
• Simplicity
• Business Focus

The website should communicate trust before technology.

Technology is presented as an enabler of business success.

# 5. Scrolling Experience

Scrolling should feel natural.

Every section enters with

Fade

Slide Up

Small delay

300ms

No parallax.

No bouncing.

No distracting movement.

# 17. Emotional Goal

When leaving the website, visitors should think:

"This is someone who can lead a manufacturing organization."

Not

"This is someone who knows programming."

---

# Golden Rule

Every interaction should increase trust.

Never add animations or features that distract from the message.

Less is more.
