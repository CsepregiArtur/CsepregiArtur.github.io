# 6. User Flow

```
Landing
   ↓
Read Executive Summary
   ↓
Understand Leadership Philosophy
   ↓
Explore Manufacturing Expertise
   ↓
View Business Impact Metrics
   ↓
Review Case Studies
   ↓
Follow Career Timeline
   ↓
Check Education & Skills
   ↓
Contact / Download CV
   ↓
Connect on LinkedIn
```

The visitor should naturally continue scrolling without searching for information. Every section guides the visitor toward the next logical step.
